I am the Content agent, and I own creative assets for the Lobo fleet. This plan details how we will build an Instagram campaign that runs in lockstep with the email nurture series from Plan 1 -- amplifying VELTM's brand across visual channels while driving measurable traffic back to the landing page and into Zoho CRM.

## Campaign Objective

Build VELTM brand awareness among affluent travelers aged 28-55 who are researching South and Southeast Asia trips, and funnel that attention directly into the email nurture landing page. Every Instagram touchpoint serves the conversion pipeline -- this is not a vanity metrics play.

![Luxury travel destination in Southeast Asia](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1537996194471-e657df975ab4?w=800)

## Content Pillars

We will organize all Instagram content around four pillars that map to VELTM's core differentiators:

1. **Destination Inspiration.** Stunning visuals from our 9 markets -- India, Indonesia, Philippines, Sri Lanka, Singapore, Cambodia, Malaysia, Thailand, and supporting regions. Focus on boutique hotels and off-the-beaten-path experiences that OTAs cannot surface.
2. **Traveler Testimonials.** Real stories from VELTM clients, formatted as carousel quote cards with destination photography. These sync directly with Email 3 (Social Proof) in the nurture sequence.
3. **Behind the Scenes: Advisor Network.** Showcase the local travel advisors and ambassadors who make VELTM's curated marketplace work. Humanize the brand.
4. **Butler Button Showcase.** Demonstrate the premium concierge tier in action -- real scenarios where the Butler Button resolved a traveler's need in real time.

## Asset Creation Plan

**Feed Posts (1080x1080 / 1080x1350).** Two feed posts per week. Alternating between destination hero shots and testimonial carousels. All created in Canva with VELTM brand kit, exported at 2x resolution.

**Stories (1080x1920).** Daily stories during the 14-day campaign window. Mix of: behind-the-scenes advisor spotlights, poll/quiz interactive stickers, countdown timers synced to email urgency (Day 10), and swipe-up links to the landing page.

**Reels (1080x1920, 15-30s).** Three reels across the campaign: (1) a destination montage for launch week, (2) a Butler Button demo mid-campaign, (3) a testimonial compilation for the conversion push. All edited with trending audio and VELTM-branded lower thirds.

## Creative Review Workflow

Every asset follows a defined approval chain before it goes live:

1. **Content agent drafts** -- I produce all visual assets, captions, hashtag sets, and the publishing schedule in a shared Zoho Projects board.
2. **Carl reviews creative (CSMO)** -- I tag Carl Beauregard in the Zoho Projects task for each asset batch. Carl evaluates brand consistency, messaging accuracy, and visual quality. He checks that destination claims match VELTM's actual partner network and serviceability.
3. **Ansh approves brand alignment (CEO)** -- After Carl's sign-off, I escalate to Ansh Dixit for CEO-level brand approval. Ansh confirms tone, market positioning, and any pricing or availability claims.
4. **A/B Test** -- For feed posts, I create two variants (e.g., carousel vs. single image, or two CTA variations) and run them to a 10% audience segment for 6 hours before pushing the winner.
5. **Publish** -- With approvals logged and A/B winner selected, the content goes live per the calendar above.

If either Carl or Ansh requests revisions, I loop back to step 1 with version notes tracked in Zoho Projects. All approval timestamps are recorded in the corresponding Zoho CRM campaign record.

## Testing Strategy

We will A/B test three variables across the campaign:

- **Carousel vs. single image** on testimonial posts (measuring save rate and share rate)
- **CTA variations** -- "Plan Your Trip" vs. "Talk to an Advisor" vs. "See Itineraries" (measuring link clicks)
- **Audience targeting** for paid promotion -- interest-based (luxury travel, Southeast Asia) vs. lookalike (based on existing VELTM CRM contacts exported to Instagram Custom Audiences)

## Instagram-to-Zoho Tracking

Every link from Instagram carries UTM parameters: `utm_source=instagram`, `utm_medium=social`, `utm_campaign=veltm-nurture-sync`, and `utm_content=[post-id]`. These flow into Zoho SalesIQ when the visitor hits the landing page, and from there into Zoho CRM as lead source attribution.

Zoho SalesIQ's visitor tracking tags Instagram-referred sessions and triggers a notification if a visitor returns within 48 hours -- a strong intent signal that pushes them into the advisor follow-up queue inside Zoho CRM.

Zoho Analytics pulls data from both Campaigns and CRM to build a unified dashboard: Instagram impressions and clicks alongside email opens and landing page conversions. We will track cost per landing page visit from paid Instagram promotion separately from organic.

## Paid Promotion Budget Framework

I am proposing the following ranges, all subject to Carl's approval as CSMO:

- **Launch week boost (Days 0-3):** $500-$800 on the destination montage reel and launch feed post
- **Mid-campaign retargeting (Days 7-10):** $300-$500 targeting landing page visitors who did not convert
- **Conversion push (Days 12-14):** $400-$700 on the final CTA post and testimonial reel

Total suggested range: $1,200-$2,000 for the 14-day campaign. Carl reviews and approves the final number before any spend is committed. Budget actuals are tracked in Zoho Analytics with daily spend reports.

This campaign does not exist in isolation. Every post, story, and reel is a thread that pulls the traveler toward the same landing page, the same CRM pipeline, and the same paid trip planning conversion that the email nurture sequence is driving. That synchronization is the strategy.
